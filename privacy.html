Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Overview

YesCoach is a strength training platform currently in pre-alpha development. Your privacy matters to us.

During pre-alpha, YesCoach does not collect, transmit, or store any personal training data.

App Data

All workout data, exercise history, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. No training information is sent to external servers.

The app uses local device storage to save:

  • Workout plans and exercise configurations
  • Set tracking and performance history
  • User preferences and settings

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted.

Email List

If you join our early access list, we collect only your email address. We use this to send occasional updates about YesCoach development. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Third-Party Services

During pre-alpha, the app does not integrate with any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Future Changes

Before public release, this privacy policy will be updated to reflect any data collection practices. Users will be notified of any changes.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through our website.

This policy applies to pre-alpha versions only.
