Privacy Policy - YesCoach

Last Updated: November 28, 2025

Overview

YesCoach is a biomechanically-intelligent strength training platform currently in internal testing.

Data Collection

During internal testing, YesCoach does not collect, transmit, or store any user data.

All workout data, exercise history, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. No information is sent to external servers.

Local Storage

The app uses local device storage to save:

  • Workout plans and exercise configurations
  • Set tracking and performance history
  • User preferences and settings

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted.

Third-Party Services

During internal testing, the app does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms.

Future Changes

Before public release, this privacy policy will be updated to reflect any data collection practices. Users will be notified of any changes.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.

Note: This policy applies to internal testing versions only.
